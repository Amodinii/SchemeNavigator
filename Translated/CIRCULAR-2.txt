The QR Code is mandatory for all farmers. Before stocking seeds at Raitha Samparka Kendras (Farmer Contact Centers), it must be ensured that the QR Code is present on the seed packets. When selling seeds, the distribution must only be done after making an entry through the QR Code in the Seed MIS. Under no circumstances should seeds without a QR Code be distributed.

**Special Instructions:**

1.  Each time seeds are distributed, they must not be distributed using the same bill version for every transaction. With this system, the details of the remaining seeds will not be available after each distribution.

3.  The subsidy amount released for seeds will be directly transferred to the farmer's account. The remaining share of the money, at a rate of 50%, is to be given to the farmer.
4.  This circular will remain in effect until further orders/notifications are issued.
5.  When stocking seeds at Raitha Samparka Kendras, they must be stocked separately for each variety. For example, when stocking groundnuts/peanuts, the R.F.L. variety should be stocked in one place and the K.C.G. variety in another, thus stocking them separately.
6.  As per government guidelines, the price for sowing seeds has been fixed. Seeds should be distributed by limiting it to a maximum of 5 acres (2 hectares) per family, for any one crop only, and with a maximum limit of 3 acres per farmer.
