### **English Translation**

For all farmers, since the QR Code is mandatory, it must be ensured that seeds are stocked at the Farmer Contact Centers and that all sales information is recorded using this QR Code. Likewise, distribution is mandatory only after the details have been recorded in the Seed MIS via the QR Code. Under no circumstances should seeds be distributed without recording the QR Code data.

**Instructions to be Followed:**

1.  While distributing certified seeds, to ensure availability for every farmer, distribute seeds for the measure of one acre only. This system does not apply to only high-quality varieties.
2.  This information must be published at the Farmer Contact Centers, and clear information should be provided to the farmers without leaving any room for confusion.
3.  While selling seeds, they must not be sold at a price higher than the fixed Maximum Retail Price (MRP). The subsidy provided to each farmer should be fixed on a per-bag basis.
4.  Disciplinary action will be taken against any officer/employee who fails to comply with the instructions of this circular and commits any lapse.
5.  The details of seed stock and sales at the Farmer Contact Centers must be updated on a daily basis. Furthermore, Taluk-wise and Farmer Contact Center-wise details of stock and sales must be provided.
6.  Stock and sales details should be provided twice a day, in the morning and evening. Overall, measures must be taken to ensure there is no confusion of any kind during seed distribution.
