### English Translation

**Biometric Report**

16. Identify the ration cards as per the specified number in each list. Take action by obtaining the biometric report from the D.C.B. accounts.

**Seed Distribution and Sales Centers:-**

17. With the objective of implementing biometrics and bringing transparency to seed distribution, the distribution of seeds shall be done exclusively through Raitha Samparka Kendras (Farmer Contact Centers) / Offices of the Additional Director of Agriculture, and seed sales centers.

18. When undertaking seed distribution at Raitha Samparka Kendras / other sales centers, the following procedures must be followed:
    a. Priority should be given to locally reputed seed distribution agencies for supplying to the Raitha Samparka Kendra.
    b. Before purchasing seeds from suppliers, priority should be given to the distribution of seeds from locally reputed brand companies.
    c. Permission shall be granted for seed distribution in identified sales centers (including permission to distribute seeds at Raitha Samparka Kendras). Government-owned organizations shall also be permitted to undertake distribution by appointing agencies.

19. Regarding seed distribution by government-owned organizations. All government-owned organizations shall be instructed to follow a single, uniform rate.
    a. Distribution must be done through agencies approved by the department and only at the rates fixed by the department.
    b. Seeds must not be distributed in the presence of any officers other than those at the additional sales centers. In the event of such a distribution, disciplinary action will be taken against the responsible officers.

20. For seed sellers wishing to obtain a new license under the biometric system, a license shall be issued after collecting a deposit of Rs. 500-00 and obtaining a report from a technical officer. Further, a fee of Rs. 100-00 shall be fixed for license renewal. The District Joint Director of Agriculture will have the authority to issue new licenses and to renew them.

21. For the management of records concerning cash transactions at seed distribution and sales centers, a Cash Bill Book must be mandatorily maintained. For transparency during distribution, the transaction between private seed sellers and farmers must be conducted with biometric authentication.
