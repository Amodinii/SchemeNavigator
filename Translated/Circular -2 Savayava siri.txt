**Government of Karnataka**
**(Department of Agriculture)**

Number: UKruNi/SaaKru/SamNee-2/2021-22
Commissionerate of Agriculture,
Sheshadri Road, Bengaluru
Date: 18.03.2022

**Circular-2**

**Subject:** Regarding the district-level Nodal Agency for the implementation of the “Savayava Siri” scheme, a program for the year 2021-22.

**Reference:**
1.  Decision of the State Level High-Power Committee on Organic Agriculture meeting held on 17.03.2022.
2.  ‘Savayava Siri’ Circular-1, dated: 09.02.2022.
3.  Government Order No: AGRI-ACT/95/2021, Bengaluru, dated: 08.02.2022.

****

With reference to the above subject, for the implementation of the 'Savayava Siri' scheme—a program announced in the 2021-22 budget—as per the Government Order cited in reference-3, directions for appointing a district-level Nodal Agency have already been issued via Circular-1, cited in reference-2.

For the smooth implementation of the scheme, the State Level High-Power (Steering) Committee on Organic Agriculture, in its meeting on 17.03.2022 (reference-1), decided to appoint the District Federations of Organic Farmers' Associations as the district-level nodal agencies. It was also decided that in districts where such federations do not exist, a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) would be selected. It is reiterated that, as per Circular-1, directions have already been issued to select these federations as the nodal agencies.

As the process and criteria for selecting eligible NGOs as district-level nodal agencies must be presented and decided upon in a district-level committee meeting, the district-level committee, chaired by the Deputy Commissioner, should discuss this matter as already instructed in Circular-1. The selection process must be transparent, and the selection must be made based on the actual costs of the activities.

Furthermore, the Non-Governmental Organization to be selected must have a successful track record of previously implementing this scheme or a similar organic program. The said organization shall be selected only if it agrees to operate based on the actual costs of the activities and adheres to the principle of economy.
